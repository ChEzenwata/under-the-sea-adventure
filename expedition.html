Deep Sea Expedition
Dive deeper into the deepest known point of the ocea on Earth

🌊 Surface Zone

0 m - Sunlight reaches the water.

🪸 Coral Reef

50 m - Home to colorful fish and corals.

🌅 Twilight Zone

200–1000 m - Very little sunlight remains.

🌑 Midnight Zone

1000–4000 m - Completely dark waters.

⚫ Abyssal Zone

4000–6000 m - Near-freezing temperatures.

🕳️ Hadal Zone / Mariana Trench

11,000 m - The deepest known part of the ocean.

Did You Know?

💡 Bioluminescence

Many deep-sea creatures produce their own light.

🌎 Unexplored Ocean

More than 80% of Earth's oceans remain unexplored.

❄️ Extreme Conditions

The deep ocean is cold, dark, and under immense pressure.
