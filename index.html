Ocean Zones

☀️ Sunlight Zone

0–200 m deep


The sunlight zone is the uppermost 200 meters of the ocean
where sunlight penetrates, supporting photosynthesis and
sustaining the majority of marine life.

🌅 Twilight Zone

200–1000 m deep


This zone is home to a diverse array of life forms,
many of which are undiscovered by science.

🌑 Midnight Zone

1000–4000 m deep

⚫ Abyssal Zone

4000–6000 m deep

🕳️ Hadal Zone

6000+ m deep
